3. 💡 The Breakthrough Business Model: AISP {#business-model}
CropFresh is NOT a commission marketplace. It's a market-maker platform built on the AISP (All-Inclusive Sourcing Price) model.

The Pricing Formula
Buyer Price (AISP) = Farmer Ask Price + Logistics Cost + Platform Margin (4–8%) + Risk Buffer (2%)
How Revenue is Generated
Stream	Details	~% Revenue
AISP Margin	4–8% dynamic markup, embedded in buyer price	60%
Logistics Commission	10–15% of hauler fee	30%
Buyer Pro Subscription	₹499–₹999/month (credit, priority delivery)	5%
Hauler Plus Subscription	₹149–₹249/month (priority routes, faster payouts)	3%
Data API + Fintech (Year 3+)	BNPL, price intelligence, enterprise white-label	2%
Why AISP Wins
🚜 Farmers: Get 100% of their asking price — zero deductions
🧾 Buyers: Landed cost 20–25% cheaper than mandi
💰 Platform: Earns 7.5% blended take-rate sustainably

7. 🤖 AI Architecture: 5-Agent System {#ai}
CropFresh runs a Multi-Agent AI system, supervised by HITL field agents:

Agent	Function
Matchmaking Engine	Matches supply ↔ demand in real time, optimizing logistics cost, grade & freshness
DPLE (Dynamic Pricing & Logistics Engine)	Calculates Buyer Price = Farmer Ask + Logistics + Margin + Risk Buffer
CV-QG (Computer Vision Quality Grader)	Grades A/B/C from smartphone photos using Vision Transformer (ViT) + YOLOv8
Voice Accessibility Agent	Voice-first UI in 10+ Indian languages; supports offline cached models
RAG Advisory Agent	Answers farmer queries with crop recommendations, pest alerts, price predictions
The HITL Data Flywheel
More Photos → Better AI → Fewer Verifications → Lower Cost → More Profit
AI is retrained weekly using agent-labeled ground truth data. Grading accuracy target: 95% by Month 12.

Dispute Resolution AI ("Diff Engine")
Compares departure photos (agent) vs. arrival photos (buyer) to determine:

"Transit Damage (Hauler liability)"
"Hidden defect (Farmer liability)"
"Post-delivery mishandling (Buyer liability)"
Result: Dispute closure in <15 minutes. Rate drops from ~15% to <2%.

10. 🔬 Quality Assurance System {#quality}
The 3-Layer Quality System
Layer 1: AI Visual Grading Engine

Uses Vision Transformer (ViT) or YOLOv8 for defect detection
Detects: color, firmness, size, defects, worm holes, bruises, ripeness
Output: Grade (A/B/C) + Confidence Score + Shelf-Life (1–5 days)
Runs in <0.5 seconds
Layer 2: HITL Agent Verification

Triggered when AI confidence <95%, or farmer claims "Grade A"
Agent physically inspects, takes Digital Twin photos
Uses "Shuffle Technique" (check 10% crates, inspect bottom layers)
Agent decision overrides AI
Layer 3: Digital Twin Creation

Includes: farmer photos, agent photos, defect close-ups, timestamps, GPS coordinates, AI annotation overlays, crate QR tag
Creates immutable pre-transport evidence record
Reduces disputes from 15–25% → <2%
Grade Standards (NHB-aligned)
Grade	Quality	Target Buyer
A	90–95% defect-free, uniform, firm	Hotels, premium retail, export
B	Minor defects, good ripeness	General retail, distribution
C	Significant defects, over-ripe	Juices, sauces, bulk processing
