# Competitor Analysis

## Direct Competitors

### DeHaat
- **Focus**: Input supply + advisory
- **Strength**: Large network (1M+ farmers), funding ($157M)
- **Weakness**: Not marketplace-focused, limited AI
- **Gap**: No voice-first Kannada interface

### Ninjacart
- **Focus**: B2B fresh produce supply chain
- **Strength**: Logistics network, unit economics
- **Weakness**: Farmer-facing features limited
- **Gap**: No AI agents, no farmer empowerment

### AgriBazaar
- **Focus**: Online mandi, commodity trading
- **Strength**: Government backing, eNAM integration
- **Weakness**: Complex UI, not farmer-friendly
- **Gap**: No Kannada voice, no AI quality grading

## Indirect Competitors
- **BigHaat**: Agri-inputs marketplace
- **FarmEasy**: Farm management SaaS
- **Gramophone**: Crop advisory
- **Cropin**: Farm data analytics (enterprise)
