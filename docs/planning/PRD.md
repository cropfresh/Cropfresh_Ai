# Product Requirements Document (PRD)
## CropFresh AI — Agricultural Marketplace Platform

### 1. Overview
CropFresh AI connects Karnataka farmers directly with buyers through AI-powered matching, pricing intelligence, and voice-first interaction in Kannada.

### 2. Problem Statement
- Farmers get 30-40% less than market price due to middlemen
- No access to real-time market data in local language
- Quality assessment is subjective and inconsistent
- No transparent price discovery mechanism

### 3. Target Users
- **Primary**: Small & marginal farmers in Karnataka (1-5 acres)
- **Secondary**: Agricultural buyers (wholesalers, retailers, processors)
- **Tertiary**: Platform administrators

### 5. Success Metrics
- 50+ farmers onboarded in beta (3 months)
- >90% agent accuracy across all tasks
- <2 second response latency
- >20% price improvement for farmers vs middlemen
