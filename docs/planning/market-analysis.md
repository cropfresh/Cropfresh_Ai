# Market Analysis — Indian AgriTech

## Market Size
- Indian agriculture: $370B+ market
- AgriTech market: ~$24B by 2025 (growing 25% CAGR)
- Karnataka agriculture: ~$20B annually

## Key Trends
- Government push for digital agriculture (eNAM, Agri Stack)
- Rising smartphone penetration among farmers (60%+)
- WhatsApp as primary communication tool for farmers
- Growing demand for organic & traceable produce

## Karnataka Focus
- Major crops: Rice, Ragi, Coconut, Areca nut, Coffee, Spices
- 160+ APMC mandis across 30 districts
- Kannada-speaking rural population: 40M+
- Average farmer landholding: 1.5 acres

## Opportunity
- 85% of farmers still rely on middlemen
- Only 10% use any digital platform for selling
- Voice-first approach needed (many farmers not literate)
- Existing platforms neglect Kannada language support
